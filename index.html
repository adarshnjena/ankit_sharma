You can find the delete option in the more menu (⋮) available on your screen while playing your video.

What are the login options available for me?

You can create your Shayar account using Google, Facebook or Phone number.

As of now, we don’t have this feature. We are working on this feature and it will be coming in very soon

How can I share a Video/Photo Shayari?

On the video/photo page you can find the share option.

How can I report offensive or questionable content?

You can find a report post option inside the more menu option. For reporting a user, you can go to the profile of the user, click on 3 dots in the toolbar and you will get the report user option.

Click on the Profile tab and then go to edit profile option. Now click on your username to edit it.

How can I edit profile information?

Click on Profile tab and then go to edit profile option. Now click on your profile pic and you will get options to change your profile pic by taking a photo or choosing from the gallery.
